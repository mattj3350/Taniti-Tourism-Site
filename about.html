Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our most frequently asked questions. If you have a question that isn't answered here, please don't hesitate to contact us!

What is the best time of year to visit Taniti?

Taniti enjoys a tropical climate, making it a great destination year-round.

What kind of outlets are available for charging electronic devices?

Taniti uses Type A and Type B outlets, which are the same as those used in the United States. The standard voltage is 110-120V, and the frequency is 60Hz. Visitors from other countries may need to bring a plug adapter or voltage converter to use their electronic devices.

What is the drinking age in Taniti?

The legal drinking age in Taniti is 18 years old.

What hours is alcohol served in Taniti?

Alcohol is not allowed to be served between the hours of 12:00 AM and 9:00 AM.

Is there an official language in Taniti?

The official language of Taniti is Tahitian, but English and French are also widely spoken, especially by younger residents.

What currency is used in Taniti?

The currency used in Taniti is the USD. Many businesses will also accept euros or yen. Most banks will facilitate currency exchange for visitors.

What are the medical facilities like in Taniti?

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics that provide medical care to residents and visitors. It is recommended to have travel insurance in case of emergencies.

What national holidays are celebrated in Taniti?

Taniti celebrates several national holidays, including Independence Day, New Year's Day, and Labor Day. Local festivals and cultural events are also held throughout the year.

Is crime a concern in Taniti?

Violent crime is relatively low in Taniti, but petty theft can occur. Visitors are advised to take standard precautions, such as securing valuables and being aware of their surroundings.
