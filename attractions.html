Things to Do

Taniti is a beautiful island with a variety of attractions to explore. From stunning beaches to lush rainforests, there is something for everyone to enjoy. Visitors can relax on the pristine shores, go snorkeling in the vibrant coral reefs, or hike through the scenic trails that wind through the island's diverse landscapes. Whether you're seeking adventure or tranquility, Taniti offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and exciting activities.

Attractions

🏝️

Beaches

Taniti boasts some of the most beautiful beaches in the Pacific, with crystal-clear waters and soft, white sand. Whether you're looking to relax under the sun or enjoy water activities like snorkeling and paddleboarding, our beaches offer something for everyone. Don't miss the chance to explore hidden coves and vibrant coral reefs just offshore.

🌿

Rainforest

The lush rainforest of Taniti is home to a diverse array of flora and fauna. Take a guided hike through the trails to discover exotic plants, colorful birds, and unique wildlife. The rainforest offers a serene escape and a chance to connect with nature in its purest form.

🌋

Volcano

Taniti's volcano is a must-see attraction for adventurous travelers. Hike to the summit for breathtaking panoramic views of the island and the surrounding ocean. The volcano's unique geological features and vibrant landscapes make it a fascinating destination for nature enthusiasts and photographers alike.

👀

Sightseeing

Taniti offers excellent sightseeing opportunities, and while most tourists spend the majority of their time in Taniti City enjoying the native architecture and nearby sandy beaches, there are also beautiful bus tours of the island and unique hikes through the rainforest.

🎭

Entertainment

Most tourists visit Taniti for its beautiful beaches, to explore the rainforest, or to visit the volcano. These aren't the only things to do however, as there are also chartered fishing tours, snorkeling, zip-lining, and several local pubs. There is also a museum, helicopter rides, an arcade, art galleries, bowling, a dance club, and movies. Many of the activities are located in the rapidly developing Merriton Landing, on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Transportation

Getting around Taniti is easy and convenient. The island offers various transportation options, including public buses running from 5 AM to 11 PM, taxi systems in Taniti City, and rental cars available for those who prefer to explore at their own pace. Additionally, there are bike rentals and walking paths for those who want to enjoy the scenic beauty of the island up close.
